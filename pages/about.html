UX & Industrial
Designer



Currently based in London, UK, Gerardo focuses on new ways to communicate and innovate through the merger of Design, Science and Technology.

He believes that by blurring the edges of the design practice, immersing oneself into multiple territories, a designer can achieve a better understanding of human needs and its surroundings which then facilitates the crafting of meaningful experiences.
